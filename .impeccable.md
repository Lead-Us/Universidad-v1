## Design Context

### Users
Chilean university students, 18–25 years old. Using the app throughout their semester to manage courses, schedules, tasks, and study with AI. High-stress context: deadlines, exams, multiple subjects at once. They use it on laptops primarily, sometimes on phones between classes.

### Brand Personality
**Focused, intelligent, student-native.**
- Not a corporate SaaS — it's a personal academic tool
- Feels like a well-designed notebook or planner brought into the digital age
- Confident without being flashy; useful without being boring

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Dark minimal — #0a0a0a background, very dark surfaces (#161616), indigo accent (#6366f1)
- **Display font**: Epilogue (Google Fonts) — tall, editorial, italic capacity; used for headings and branding moments
- **UI font**: DM Sans — established system font, used for all body/UI text
- **Background texture**: Subtle dot grid (radial-gradient pattern), not blobs or gradients
- **Feature layouts**: Numbered editorial rows preferred over icon card grids
- **Motion**: Subtle entrance animations (fadeIn + translateY/X), pulse on live indicators
- **No gradient text** — solid colors only (accent #6366f1 for emphasis, white for primary)
- **No glassmorphism** — surfaces are solid dark backgrounds with subtle borders
- **Anti-reference**: Generic Tailwind SaaS dark dashboards with cyan glows and purple-blue gradients

### Design Principles
1. **Structured over decorative** — every element earns its place; no decoration for its own sake
2. **Typography-first hierarchy** — use Epilogue at display weights to establish clear section rhythm
3. **Restrained color** — accent color is used sparingly; most UI is achromatic with light borders
4. **Editorial layouts** — prefer numbered rows, asymmetry, and horizontal bands over card grids
5. **Motion with purpose** — entrance animations only where they signal something meaningful; no idle animations except status indicators
